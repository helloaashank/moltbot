# SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION

## OpenWhen Letters

### Digital time-locked messages for the people you love

**What it does:**
1. Create a letter with an "Open when..." title
2. Optionally set a future unlock date
3. Share via a beautiful, permanent link
4. Receiver sees a countdown until unlock date
5. Letter unlocks forever after that date

**Why it's different:**

| Feature | OpenWhen | FutureMe | Text/Email | Physical Letters |
