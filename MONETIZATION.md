## 1. Premium Subscriptions (Recurring Revenue)

### Tier Structure

| Feature | Free | Premium ($4.99/mo) | Premium Annual ($39.99/yr) |
|---------|------|-------------------|---------------------------|
| Text letters | ✅ Unlimited | ✅ Unlimited | ✅ Unlimited |
| Date locking | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Shareable links | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Photo attachments | ❌ | ✅ (5/letter) | ✅ (unlimited) |
| Voice notes | ❌ | ✅ (2 min) | ✅ (10 min) |
| Video messages | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ (30 sec) |
| Premium templates | ❌ | ✅ (20+) | ✅ (all) |
| Custom themes | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Scheduled delivery | ❌ | ✅ (email) | ✅ (email + SMS) |
| Letter analytics | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ (views, opens) |
| Priority support | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

### Why This Works

- **Free tier is fully functional** → No barrier to viral growth
- **Premium enhances emotion** → Photos of you, voice of you
- **Couples share cost** → One subscription, both use
- **Annual discount (33% off)** → Improves cash flow, reduces churn

### Projection (Conservative)

| Month | Active Users | Conversion | Paid Users | MRR |
|-------|--------------|------------|------------|-----|
| 3 | 5,000 | 2% | 100 | $500 |
| 6 | 25,000 | 3% | 750 | $3,750 |
| 12 | 100,000 | 4% | 4,000 | $20,000 |
| 18 | 250,000 | 5% | 12,500 | $62,500 |

## 2. One-Time Purchases (Low Friction)

### Product Ideas

| Product | Price | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| **Memory Vault** | $19.99 | Create 10 letters with photos, lifetime access |
| **Anniversary Pack** | $14.99 | 5 premium templates + scheduled delivery |
| **Long-Distance Bundle** | $24.99 | Unlimited media for 30 days |
| **Grief Support Pack** | $12.99 | Templates for loss, memorial letters |
| **Parent-to-Child** | $29.99 | 18 letters (age 1-18), photo support |

### Why One-Time Works

- Some users **hate subscriptions**
- Perfect for **single use cases** (anniversary, birthday)
- Lower commitment → More conversions
- Can upsell to subscription later

### Projection

- 3-5% of free users make one-time purchase
- Average order value: $18
- Month 12: 100,000 users × 4% × $18 = **$72,000 one-time revenue**

## 3. B2B / Enterprise (High Margin)

### Opportunity A: White-Label for Greeting Companies

**Target:** Hallmark, Moonpig, Blue Mountain

**Offer:** License your date-locking technology

**Pricing:**
- Setup fee: $25,000
- Monthly: $5,000-15,000 (based on volume)
- Revenue share: 10% of premium conversions

**Pitch:** "Add time-locked letters to your existing greeting card app"

### Opportunity B: Therapy & Wellness Apps

**Target:** BetterHelp, Talkspace, Calm, Headspace

**Use case:** Therapists assign "future self" letters as homework

**Pricing:**
- Per-therapist license: $29/mo
- Unlimited letters for their clients
- HIPAA-compliant option: +$100/mo

### Opportunity C: Wedding & Event Platforms

**Target:** The Knot, Zola, WeddingWire

**Use case:** Couples write letters to open on anniversaries

**Pricing:**
- Bundled with premium wedding packages
- $2-5 per couple (volume licensing)

## 4. Physical Products (High Margin)

### Product: "QR Code Letter Cards"

**What:** Physical card with QR code + beautiful design

**Use case:** 
- Sender orders physical card
- Card mailed to receiver
- QR code links to digital letter
- Best of both worlds: physical + digital

**Pricing:**
- Single card: $8.99 + shipping
- 5-pack: $34.99 + shipping
- 12-pack: $79.99 + shipping

**Fulfillment:**
- Print-on-demand (Printful, Gooten)
- No inventory risk
- ~60% gross margin

### Product: "Open When Letter Kits"

**What:** Box with 20 physical envelopes + instructions + digital access

**Pricing:** $49.99 + shipping

**Margin:** ~50% after production + shipping

## 5. Advertising (Free Tier Monetization)

**Strategy:** Minimal, non-intrusive ads for free users

**Placement:**
- Small banner on "Create Letter" page
- No ads on "View Letter" experience (sacred)
- Native ads: "Send a physical card" upsell

**Revenue:**
- CPM: $2-5 (niche audience = higher rates)
- 100,000 monthly pageviews × $3 CPM = **$300/mo**

**Note:** This is small revenue but helps cover hosting costs for free users.

## Funding Strategy

### Bootstrap Phase (Months 1-6)
- Revenue: $5K-20K/mo
- Team: You + 1 contractor
- Goal: Prove product-market fit

### Pre-Seed (Months 7-12)
- Raise: $150K-300K
- Use: Hire 2 engineers, marketing
- Goal: Scale to $50K+ MRR

### Seed (Months 13-24)
- Raise: $1M-2M
- Use: Full team, B2B sales, international
- Goal: Scale to $500K+ MRR

### Exit Opportunities
- **Acquisition:** Greeting card company ($10-50M)
- **Acquisition:** Messaging app ($20-100M)
- **Acquisition:** Wellness platform ($15-75M)
- **Remain profitable:** $2-5M/year owner cash flow
