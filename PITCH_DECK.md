# OpenWhen Letters
### Digital Time-Capsule Messages for the People You Love

---

## 🎯 The Problem

**"Open When" letters are a beautiful tradition** — handwritten notes sealed in envelopes with instructions like:
- "Open when you're sad"
- "Open when you miss me"
- "Open on our anniversary"

**But they have limitations:**
- Physical letters can get lost or damaged
- Hard to create many at once
- No reminder system
- Can't include photos, videos, or music
- Awkward to store and organize

---

## 💡 The Solution

**OpenWhen Letters** — A beautiful, minimal web app that lets you create digital "Open When" letters with:

- ✨ **Date-locked delivery** — Set a future date when the letter unlocks
- 🔗 **Shareable links** — One link per letter, works forever
- 🎨 **Beautiful design** — Soft, emotional aesthetics that feel special
- 💾 **No accounts needed** — Create and share in seconds
- 🌍 **Accessible anywhere** — Works on any device with a browser

---

## 🎁 The Product

### For Senders
1. Click "Create Letter"
2. Write your message ("Open when...")
3. **Optional:** Select an unlock date from a calendar
4. Get a shareable link
5. Send via text, email, or social media

### For Receivers
1. Receive a link
2. See a beautiful preview: *"You've received something special"*
3. **If locked:** "This letter unlocks on [date] — come back then!"
4. **If unlocked:** Read the letter, save it, share it
